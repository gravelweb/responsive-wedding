QUESTIONS

Is the wedding outdoors?

Yes, everything will take place outdoors. While the reception will take place under a tent with a dance floor, the rest will be on grass, so watch your shoes!

Where can I park?

There is lots of parking at the venue - and signs to direct you from there to the party! If you are parking your car overnight, please be sure to pick it up by 10 the next morning.

What time does it end?

We have the property until midnight but, due to Ontario's By-law restrictions, all amplified music must be turned off at 11pm. Don't worry! We'll have acoustic guitars and fire pits to end the night off nice and cozy for those that wanna stick around with us till the end.
