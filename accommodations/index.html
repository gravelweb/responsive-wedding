DETAILS

The hotels listed here have a number of blocked rooms with discounted rates for guests of our Wedding. Just make sure to reference the Gravel-Pace wedding for the discounted rate.

Depending on the number of bookings at the Hampton Inn hotel, we may be able to organize a shuttle service to and from the wedding.

For guests that only need to be accommodated the night of the wedding, we recommend the Best Western Colonel Butler Inn. If you stay here, we recommend that you arrive ready, since check-in at the hotel is not guaranteed until 3:00 PM (though we have made a special request for check-in to be at 2:00 PM). This option is most ideal for those who live in the GTA and need a place to pass out after the party!

Please keep in mind that the hotels will release all rooms that remain unclaimed back to the general public after July 31st (one month before the wedding), so don’t wait too long!

OTHER OPTIONS

B&B’s + SHORT-TERM VACATION RENTALS

For anyone who would like to take advantage of their time in NOTL and experience something a little different from the usual chain hotel, we highly recommend looking into the many B&B’s or Small Property Rentals in the area. While chain hotel options are somewhat limited, Niagara-On-The-Lake has over 167 Bed and Breakfasts to choose from! Property Rentals are also great options for groups – split amongst friends, many of these come to the same price as a hotel room.

We’ve listed a few websites to help you on your search!

  • Chamber of Commerce
  • VRBO
  • Booking.com
  • Niagara Smart Stays
  • Niagara Holiday Rentals
